About Sharon

Sharon Kaminski

Sharon Kaminski has been bringing her vast knowledge of Pilates and group fitness to the Grand Strand since 2004. Known for her energetic personality and unparalleled ability to reach students of all levels, Sharon is constantly developing innovative ways to incorporate balls, weights, Pilates Rings and stretch bands into her classes.

IFTA and AFFA certified, Sharon not only teaches group fitness but is also a certified personal trainer. She is grateful to have even more of an opportunity to help her students reach their fitness goals.

Meet Sharon Kaminski

Sharon Kaminski, owner of Custom Fitness with Sharon LLC, grew up in New Jersey. At the age of 4, Betty and John Kaminski enrolled their daughter in her first dance class. Her training at Dance World Academy led her to many accomplishments, including being a Star Search Grand Final Champion. Not only did they teach her great technique but also encouraged her to never give up and love the artistry of dance!

After graduating from Point Park University with a BA in Dance, Sharon performed in Nashville at Opryland and the General Jackson Showboat, Fiesta Texas in San Antonio and the Shoji Tabuchi Show in Branson. She also appeared at the Carousel Dinner Theater in Akron, Ohio and was the dance captain on the Century with Celebrity Cruises.

After sailing the high seas, Sharon came to Myrtle Beach, SC and performed at the Alabama Theatre for 14 years and retired at the end of December of 2014. While working there she started teaching Pilates at Golds Gym in North Myrtle Beach and became a Certified Group Fitness Instructor. As her love of fitness grew, Sharon became a Certified Personal Trainer. She loves her group exercise classes but also enjoys working one on one to customize fitness programs to help each individual reach their goal. No matter what fitness level, experience, or age, Custom Fitness with Sharon can fine tune your fitness to fit your lifestyle.

Dance will always be a part of Sharon's life and anyone who takes her classes can still see the joy in her heart for dance. However, her passion now, has turned to helping people become more fit and enjoying life to its fullest!
